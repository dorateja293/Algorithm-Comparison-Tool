Algorithm Comparison Tool

Compare different algorithms for Knapsack and Traveling Salesman Problems

Welcome

This tool implements and compares various algorithms:

  • Greedy: Fast but may not be optimal
  • Dynamic Programming: Optimal solutions with higher time complexity
  • Backtracking: Brute force approach
  • Branch & Bound: Optimized search with bounds
  • Divide & Conquer: Recursive splitting

Problems solved:

  • 0/1 Knapsack: Select items to maximize value without exceeding weight capacity
  • TSP: Find the shortest tour visiting each city once
